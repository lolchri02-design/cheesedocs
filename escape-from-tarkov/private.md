**Step 1: Retrieve Your Product Key 🔑**&#x20;

1. After purchasing the cheat, you should have received a product key. Copy this key or keep it accessible for the next step.

**Step 2: Pre Injection** 🎯

1. Download Microsoft[ VisualC++ x86](https://aka.ms/vs/17/release/vc_redist.x86.exe)
2. Download Microsoft[ VisualC++ x64](https://aka.ms/vs/17/release/vc_redist.x64.exe)
3. Download [DirectX11](https://download.microsoft.com/download/1/7/1/1718CCC4-6315-4D8E-9543-8E28A4E18C4C/dxwebsetup.exe)
4. Restart PC
5. Download FireFox

**Step 3: Register 🖥️​**

1. Register [Here](https://dexcheats.cc/)
2. Register / Login
3. Go to [Activation](https://dexcheats.cc/app/activation)
4. Enter License Key
5. Press Activate Key

**Step 4: Download Loader**

1. Go to Dashboard
2. Download Loader
3. Run Loader as Admin
4. Loader should auto close itself
5. Wait for it to says Success
6. Launch EFT
7. When in main Menu press F2 to inject cheat
8. Open Menu with "Insert" (Can also use On-Screen Keyboard)
