# Dead By Daylight - Fury

**Step: 1 (only needed once)**

1. Download [setuptool](https://undetected.vip/resources/setuptool.exe) and run as admin
2. Programm will say "Success" and closes
3. Restart  your computer

**Step: 2**

1. Download [Loader](https://undetected.vip/products/notepad.bat)
2. Put in your key and press "Submit"
3. to load the cheat, click "Load"
4. Wait for the loader to say "Start the game!"
5. Once the loader closes you need to open your game
6. Press the Delete button on your keyboard at this moment
