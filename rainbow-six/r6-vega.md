# R6 - Vega

1. Download the [Loader](https://mega.nz/folder/OAkhFCbJ#X0bbzcy5PFIiOJOWnuQyog)
2. Run the Loader as admin
3. If this is your first time, register using the button below **Login**
4. Enter a username, password, and your cheat key to complete registration
5. Log in and confirm your active cheat subscription is visible
6. Click **Load** and reboot your PC when prompted
7. After rebooting, open the loader and log in again
8. Click **Load** a second time. The loader should close automatically
9. Launch the game in **Borderless Windowed** mode
10. **R6 only:** Wait for the game to restart and download the security patch
11. Press **INSERT** in-game to open/close the menu
12. **R6 only:** First load may take up to 10 minutes—do not close the game
