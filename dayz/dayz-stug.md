# DayZ - Stug

**Step 1: Loader Setup 🖥️​**

1. Download the [Loader](https://mega.nz/folder/QCExhQKZ#JIAUUwlooJxl9RBQBP8Djw)
2. Unzip Loader from the .Rar
3. Run loader as Admin
4. Enter your key
5. Open your game
6. Click enter to Load Dayz
7. A pop-up will appear asking about injection. Click OK to continue
8. Press Insert to open the menu in-game
